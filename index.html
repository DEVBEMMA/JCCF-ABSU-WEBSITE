About JCCF ABSU

The spiritual umbrella body uniting all Christian fellowships and denominations at ABSU

The Joint Christian Campus Fellowship (JCCF) serves as the spiritual umbrella body of all Christian fellowships and church denominations within Abia State University. We represent the unity of the Body of Christ on campus, bringing together diverse Christian communities under one vision.

Our foundation is built on the infallibility of Scripture and the doctrine of Christ. We believe in fostering unity among all Christian students while maintaining our individual denominational identities and traditions.

Through JCCF, fellowships like TACSFON, NIFES, CASOR, SUCF, RCF, and many others come together in unity to advance the Kingdom of God on campus and impact our university community with the love of Christ.

Our Core Beliefs

We believe in the infallibility of Scripture, the deity of Christ, salvation by grace through faith, and the unity of all believers in the Body of Christ. Our mission is to demonstrate this unity practically on campus.
